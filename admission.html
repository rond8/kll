Admission Procedure

College Admission Test (KLL-CAT)

  1. 1

    From the Admission and Testing Office (ATO), secure and fill out the Application form.

  2. 2

    Submit all the required documents at (ATO) for verification.

  3. 3

    Wait for the schedule of KLL-CAT from the ATO's assigned Personnel.

  4. 4

    Take the test as scheduled, and wait for the announcement of the result via KLL Official facebook page.

  5. 5

    If passed, secure the Admission Slip given to qualified applicants from the ATO.

  6. 6

    Proceed to the Registrar's Office for enrollment as scheduled.

Admission Requirements

  • • Accomplished Application Form
  • •
    Photocopy of SHS Report Card (Form 138) - Passed, Preferably with a grade point average of 80% (FOR NEW ENROLLEES)
  • •
    Photocopy of Transcript of Records (TOR) (FOR TRANSFEREES)
  • • Photocopy of Certificate of Good Moral Character (No pending legal, moral, and ethical issues)
  • • Photocopy of Birth Certificate (PSA)
  • • Applicant's / Parent's Identification Card (ID)
  • • 2 pcs. of 1x1 Recent ID picture with white background
  • • 2 pcs. of 2x2 Recent ID picture with white background
  • • Long Brown Envelope

Additional Requirements for Bachelor of Science in Nursing (BSN)

  • •
    Certificate of Indigency (low-income family) issued by a veritable and authorized Barangay Official, bearing official seal. (Note: only 1 applicant from 1 family shall be accommodated to give equal opportunities.)
  • • Certification coming from the last school attended that applicant belongs to the upper 40% of the graduating class and a GWA of 90% or above with no grades below 90%
  • • Proof of graduation from DepEd - accredited school, graduated from any strand at the Senior High School program.
