Admission Requirements

To be admitted to Kolehiyo ng Lungsod ng Lipa, applicants must meet the following requirements:

  • High school diploma or equivalent
  • Completed application form
  • Transcript of records
  • Birth certificate
  • Medical certificate
  • Two (2) passport-sized photos
  • Barangay clearance

Application Process

The admission process involves the following steps:

  1. Submit completed application form and requirements to the Admissions Office
  2. Take the entrance examination
  3. Interview with the Admissions Committee
  4. Receive notification of acceptance
  5. Enroll and pay fees

Important Dates

Please note the following important dates for admission:

  • Application Period: January to March
  • Entrance Examination: April
  • Interview: May
  • Enrollment: June
